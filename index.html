WELCOME TO COOL APP

COOL APP works as a webapp on mobile devices.

Mobile Support (Recommended)

on iOS, press the "Share" icon, then "Add to Home Screen" to use COOL APP.

on Android, open Chrome, click the 3 dots on the URL bar, and press "Open COOL APP".


PC Support (Beta)

on PC, open Chrome then click the Install icon (which resembles a down-arrow within a monitor).
